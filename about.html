About us

Mac-Geoid Western Nig. Limited

is an indigenous private limited liability company based in Port Harcourt, the Rivers State capital. It was registered in nigeria under corporate affairs commission as a business entity. We are actively engaged in providing quality services to our clients with innovative technical solutions to the oil and gas industry in nigeria As an established company, Mac-Geoid Western Nig Ltd has a wide range of capabilities in many services with adequate machinery and equipment to ensure accuracy, quality and rapid achievement of work.

Environmental Protection and Safety

Mac-Geoid Western Nig Limited lays primary emphasis on the safety of its personnel and the environment and therefore has a Community affairs, security, health, environmental and safety (CASHES) manager also known as the HSE (Health, Safety and Environment) manager.
Our specific goal under our CASHES Policy and HSE management is to assist clients and customers in their efforts to minimize both quantity and severity of none productive time and to provide options with which an clients and customers can successfully reduce cost and liabilities associated with the project.
